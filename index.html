About Me


Hi! I’m Veronique, a graphic designer from Canada with a strong interest in UX. I graduated from a three-year graphic design program in Québec and recently completed an undergraduate degree with a concentration in UX, strengthening my understanding of user-centered design.


Empathy plays a big role in how I approach design. I focus on creating visually pleasing, accessible and easy-to-use experiences.


I’m always looking to grow by learning new tools, refining my process and finding better ways to design with people in mind.

I design thoughtful, user-centered digital experiences that make people’s lives easier.
